How to Use Income Calculator

  1. Income Type: Select the frequency of income (Annual, Monthly, Weekly, Hourly).
  2. Hourly/Weekly Income: If hourly or weekly, enter the hourly wage and hours worked per week.
  3. Gross Income: Enter the gross income amount based on the selected frequency.
  4. Family Size: Enter the number of people in the household (1-8+).
  5. Calculate: Click "Calculate" to see the income percentage and fee scale tier.
  6. Coverage Table: View the Federal Poverty Guidelines table to see where the income falls.
  7. Fee Scale Cards: Review the three fee scale tiers (Tier A, B, C) showing what services are covered and at what rate.
  8. Add Household Members: Click to add additional household members with their own income calculations.

Tip: The calculator automatically converts all income types to annual income and compares against Federal Poverty Guidelines!
