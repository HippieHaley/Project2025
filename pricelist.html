How to Use Pricelist

  1. Search: Use the search box to find services by name or keywords.
  2. Percent Selector: Choose the fee scale percentage (100%, 75%, 50%, 25%) to adjust prices.
  3. Show/Hide Codes: Toggle visibility of CPT, HCPCS, ICD-10, and Modifier codes.
  4. Adjustments: Click "Adjustments" to add or subtract amounts from the total. Enter a positive number to add (e.g., 10.00) or a negative number to subtract (e.g., -5.00). You can add an optional note to describe the adjustment.
  5. Add to Receipt: Click the "+" button next to any service to add it to your receipt.
  6. Receipt Panel: View selected services and adjustments on the right side (hovers at 50% opacity, becomes solid when hovering).
  7. Adjust Quantity: Change the quantity for each service item in the receipt using the dropdown.
  8. Remove Items: Click "Remove" to delete services or adjustments from the receipt.
  9. Copy Receipt: Click "Copy Receipt" to copy all selected services, adjustments, and their codes to clipboard.
  10. Categories: Services are organized by type: Visit Codes, Procedures, Labs, and Medications.

Tip: The receipt calculates totals automatically including all adjustments and shows all relevant medical codes for billing!
