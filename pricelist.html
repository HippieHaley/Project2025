How to Use Pricelist

  1. Search: Use the search box to find services by name or keywords.
  2. Percent Selector: Choose the fee scale percentage (100%, 75%, 50%, 25%) to adjust prices.
  3. Show/Hide Codes: Toggle visibility of CPT, HCPCS, ICD-10, and Modifier codes.
  4. Add to Receipt: Click the "+" button next to any service to add it to your receipt.
  5. Receipt Panel: View selected services on the right side (hovers at 50% opacity, becomes solid when hovering).
  6. Adjust Quantity: Change the quantity for each item in the receipt.
  7. Remove Items: Click "Remove" to delete items from the receipt.
  8. Copy Receipt: Click "Copy Receipt" to copy all selected services and their codes to clipboard.
  9. Categories: Services are organized by type: Visit Codes, Procedures, Labs, and Medications.

Tip: The receipt calculates totals automatically and shows all relevant medical codes for billing!
